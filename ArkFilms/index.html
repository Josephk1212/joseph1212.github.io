    Services

    Check our Services

    From creative brainstorming through post-production, our streamlined processes ensure that our clients and partners receive an effortless, personalized experience each and every time.

    Creative Concepting

    A creative concept is an overarching “Big Idea” that captures audience interest, influences their emotional response and inspires them to take action.

    Sound Design

    Sound design is how filmmakers flesh out the aural world of a film to enhance the mood, atmosphere, and/or tone.

    Video Editing

    We undertake all of the tasks associated with cutting raw footage, assembling that footage, adding music, dubbing, sound effects, just to name a few.

    Scripting & Voiceover

    The relationship between voice actors and their voiceover scripts goes beyond words; it’s about creating visual images for the listeners by emphasizing keywords, using appropriate pauses, and applying the correct tone.

    Stock Footage

    Stock footage is beneficial to filmmakers as it saves shooting new material.

    Storyboarding

    A visual representation of how a story will play out, scene by scene.
